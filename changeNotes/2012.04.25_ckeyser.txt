Subject:Species code changes
Name:Alaska cedar scientific name change
Date:04.25.2012
Variants affected:All

Description:The International Botanical Congress updated the scientific name
of Alaska cedar to Callitropsis nootkatensis (Plants symbol=CANO9) from 
Chamaecyparis nootkatensis (Plants symbol=CHNO)in the summer of 2011. The 
plants symbol has been updated in the appropriate variants and in the species
translator.


Impact on users:User's will be able to use the new PLANTS symbol to identify
                Alaska cedar.
