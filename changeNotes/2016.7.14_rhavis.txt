Subject:Volume Equations
Name: R. Havis
Date: July 14, 2016
Variants affected: CR

Description:

CR variant: New default volume equations were programmed for the Santa Fe NF.
            The new equations are for Ponderosa pine (301FW2W122), Douglas-fir
            (301FW2W202), White fir (301FW2W015), and White pine 301FW2W108).

Impact on users: Volumes for Ponderosa pine, Douglas-fir, White fir and White pine
                 will be different in the Santa Fe NF using the updated software.
